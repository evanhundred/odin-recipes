Field Greens with Salt and Pepper Vinaigrette

A simple natural taste

Mix of field greens, tossed with a light vinagrette. The perfect compliment to any hot delicious dinner or lunch plate.

Ingredients

  • Field greens mix
  • Extra Virgin Olive Oil (EVOO)
  • Balsamic Vinegar
  • 1 carrot, chopped
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Rinse and dry greens mix. Add to bowl.
  2. In a separate container, mix olive oil and vinaigrette. Add to greens bowl
  3. Chop carrot, add to bowl.
  4. Add salt and pepper to taste.
