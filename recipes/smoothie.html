Green Protein Smoothie

A naturally sweet blend of plant based protein and greens and fruits

This smoothie packs a protein punch, with peanut butter, kale, and fruit for fiber and nutrients.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cups Milk; subsitute oat milk for vegan version
  • 1 tbsp Peanut Butter
  • 1/4 cup oats
  • handful of frozen chopped mango
  • Banana

Steps

  1. Add ingredients to blender.
  2. Blend.
  3. Pour into glass and enjoy.
