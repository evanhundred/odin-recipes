Rotini with Sardines Marinara

A delicious dish with deep umami flavor, packed with protein

This simple pasta dish is a perfect mix of flavors. Light, fruity tomato sauce combines with small but dense, salty and satisfying sardines, creating a complete flavor to complement al dente spiral rotini noodles. Serve with a light salad or protein option. Pair with a class of red wine.

Ingredients

  • Rotini pasta
  • Tomato sauce
  • Sardines
  • Garlic
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Cook noodles in boiling water for 7 minutes, until al dente.
  2. In a separate pan, heat olive oil and garlic for 2 minutes.
  3. Add sardines and cook for 5 minutes.
  4. Add tomato sauce on medium heat for 5 minutes, then set heat to low and add drained pasta.
  5. Cook on low, stirring, for 2 minutes.
  6. Add sardines and cook for 5 minutes.
  7. Add tomato sauce on medium heat for 5 minutes, then set heat to low and add drained pasta.
  8. Add salt and pepper.
  9. Remove from heat and let sit for 5 minutes.
